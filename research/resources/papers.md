### 1. AgentSpec: Runtime Constraints for LLM Agents

**Title:** AgentSpec: A Domain-Specific Language for Runtime Constraints on LLM Agents

- Runtime enforcement mechanisms
- Safety boundary definitions
- Structured rules with triggers, predicates, enforcement

**Relevance:**
- Critical for AI agent safety
